### Ship v1.5.2: The Command Palette & Neural Feedback

Today's update is all about **unobtrusive power**. One of the biggest pieces of feedback I received was that the HUD was too "busy" and took up too much space. In **v1.5.2**, I've completely reimagined the interaction model.

**The Command Palette Experience:**
- The HUD is now **hidden by default**. It only descends from the top of the screen when you trigger the `Neural Uplink` (Ctrl+Shift+V).
- Inspired by tools like **Raycast** and **Spotlight**, the UI is centered and clean, allowing you to focus purely on the task at hand.
- Once an order is placed, the palette gracefully exits, keeping your screen clutter-free.

**Neural Feedback Visualizer:**
- Added a **Voice Visualizer**! Now, when you speak, you'll see real-time pink (`#F567D7`) bars pulsing to your voice. No more wondering if the mic is picking you up—the extension now "breathes" with you.
- Improved the **interim results logic**, making the transition from speech to task smoother and faster.

This version moves VTM from being a "widget" to becoming a **workflow**. It's invisible when you don't need it and powerful when you do.

_Stay focused. Speak clearly. Ship everything._ 🚀
